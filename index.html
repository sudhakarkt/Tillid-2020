Case Studies

Designed and developed a functionally rich Android App for an international football stadium, primarily conceived to manage and maintain the activities of the Stewards. This app helps the Stewards during a match day, to report incidents, raise alarms, call for help etc., in a way to make faster communications with the control room and reduce drastically the response time for any action to be taken for critical incidents/accidents. It also basically manages the reporting times of the stewards, tracks their location etc and syncs them real-time with the control room.
Created iOS & Android native apps to support a Cloud/SaaS based Expense Management solution, which integrates with mid-size enterprise solutions like Dynamics GP/AX. These Apps' main purpose is to capture expenses on the fly by taking picture of the receipts from within the apps and record them as expenses, synching them real-time (and asynchronously) to the Cloud database. Different user roles are managed for recording expenses, approvals, reporting etc within the app.
